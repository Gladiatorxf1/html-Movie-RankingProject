The Best Movies According to me are

My top Movies

Tumbad

Tumbbad (2018) is a hidden gem of Indian cinema, blending folk horror with dark fantasy. Set in the 1920s, it follows a family obsessed with unearthing a hidden fortune in a decaying mansion. Their greed unleashes an ancient, monstrous entity named Hastar, who guards the treasure. The film is known for its stunning visuals, chilling atmosphere, and exploration of human greed and its consequences.

Dhadak

Dhadak (2018) is a star-crossed love story! Across social divides in Rajasthan, Madhukar and Parthavi fight for their love against societal pressure. This Bollywood romance is all about defying expectations and the power of young love.

3 Idiots

A comedy-drama about two friends who set out to find a third friend from college and revisit their days at the prestigious Indian Institute of Engineering. It criticizes the education system in India.
